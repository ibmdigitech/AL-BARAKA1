ABOUT THE CORPORATE

High-Trust Project
Support Solutions.

Al Baraka Diyar Project Support FZE LLC is a UAE-based project support solutions provider offering comprehensive services for construction sites, solar power plants, and industrial operations. With a strong operational presence across UAE, Qatar, and KSA, we specialize in manpower support, equipment rental, transportation, and material trading.
